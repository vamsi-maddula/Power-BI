This project is a complete end-to-end Power BI Sales Analytics Dashboard developed to analyze business performance through interactive visualizations and data-driven insights. The dashboard includes Executive Overview, Revenue & Margin Analysis, and Product & Customer Analysis pages, each designed with KPI cards, trend charts, breakdown visuals, variance indicators, slicers, and business insight sections. The project demonstrates data cleaning using Power Query, data modeling with fact and dimension tables, DAX measure creation, time intelligence calculations, drill-through analysis, tooltip pages, bookmarks, navigation buttons, and interactive filtering. Key business metrics such as Total Revenue, Gross Profit, Gross Margin %, Total Orders, and Average Order Value were calculated using DAX to provide meaningful insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends. The dashboard also enables users to drill from executive-level summaries into underperforming regions, low-margin products, and customer-level analysis, creating a complete business intelligence reporting experience commonly used in real-world enterprise analytics solutions.
